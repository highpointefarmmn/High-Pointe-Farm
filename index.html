About High Pointe Farm

High Pointe Farm is a premium full-care boarding facility in Maple Plain, Minnesota, serving riders in eventing and dressage disciplines. Centered on exceptional horse care and a professionally managed, well-appointed environment, the farm provides consistent, attentive daily care within a structured and respectful barn community where horses thrive and riders feel supported.

Barn & Amenities

Our heated 22-stall barn features spacious 10×12 stalls, a large tack room, on-site laundry, and an indoor wash stall with hot water to ensure comfort and convenience year-round.

Arenas & Riding Spaces

Ride year-round in the 70×120 heated indoor arena or take advantage of the 100×200 outdoor arena. A 4-acre cross-country field supports conditioning and schooling.

Turnout & Pasture

Turnout is a priority. Horses benefit from 8 individual 40×40 turnouts, 2 paired 60×60 turnouts, and 20 acres of pasture turnout.

Location & Access

On-site trailer parking is available on a flat gravel lot for easy loading and unloading. The farm is conveniently located near Baker Park Horse Trails for additional conditioning and recreational riding.

High standards, quality facilities, and a shared passion for horsemanship define the culture of the farm. We are committed to providing exceptional care within a community that values discipline, professionalism, and the development of both horse and rider.

Full Care Boarding

$1,500 per month • Due on the 15th of each month. High Pointe Farm delivers reliable, high-quality daily care with a focus on horse welfare, communication, and consistency.

Daily Horse Care

  • Twice-daily grain feeding (AM/PM)
  • Administration of owner-provided supplements
  • Daily turnout (weather and footing permitting)
  • Blanketing and unblanketing as needed
  • Fly masks and seasonal gear as needed

Wellness Oversight

  • Daily visual wellness checks
  • Injury monitoring and prompt communication
  • Coordination with farrier and veterinarian
  • Holding for scheduled farrier and veterinary appointments

Trainer Policy

Boarders are welcome to bring their own trainer, subject to prior farm approval.

Trainer Requirements

  • Receive approval from the farm
  • Provide proof of current liability insurance
  • Sign a High Pointe Farm Trainer Agreement
  • Adhere to all farm safety standards, including helmet policies

Preferred Connections

High Pointe Farm maintains relationships with a select group of respected professionals in dressage, hunter/jumper, and cross-country disciplines. For boarders seeking training support, we are pleased to facilitate introductions to trusted trainers whose standards align with the farm’s commitment to quality, safety, and horsemanship.
