A Farm Built Around Exceptional Care

High Pointe Farm is a 22-stall full-care boarding facility serving eventing and dressage riders who care deeply about their horses and their progress. We combine attentive daily management with a supportive, welcoming barn environment where competitive junior riders and adult amateurs train with purpose.

Here, high standards and a positive community go hand in hand.

Thoughtful, Consistent Daily Care

Each horse is managed with care and attention to detail, supporting long-term soundness, comfort, and performance.

Daily Horse Care

  • Twice-daily grain feeding (AM/PM)
  • Administration of owner-provided supplements
  • Daily turnout (weather and footing permitting)
  • Blanketing and unblanketing as needed
  • Fly masks and seasonal gear as needed

Wellness Oversight

  • Daily visual wellness checks
  • Injury monitoring and prompt communication
  • Coordination with farrier and veterinarian
  • Holding for scheduled farrier and veterinary appointments

Flexible Training, Supportive Environment

Boarders are welcome to bring their own trainer, subject to prior farm approval.

Trainer Requirements

  • Receive approval from the farm
  • Provide proof of current liability insurance
  • Sign a High Pointe Farm Trainer Agreement
  • Adhere to all farm safety standards, including helmet policies

Preferred Connections

High Pointe Farm maintains relationships with respected professionals in eventing, dressage, and hunter/jumper disciplines. For boarders seeking training support, we are happy to provide introductions when requested.
